About RealITy

What is RealITy

RealITy Festival or RealITy is an annual tech rally organized by LINK.exe under the Pillar of Program that features innovative speakers from the tech industry, and emerging cutting-edge technology. Over the years, Reality Festival has served as a platform where technology trends intersect with society's fingertips, offering opportunities for students and professionals to satisfy their curiosity about the latest advancements in the industry.

RealITy XIII: Metropolis

Now on its 13th year, RealITy shines the spotlight on smart cities with the theme RealITy XIII: METROPOLIS. Inspired by Iloilo City’s ongoing digital transformation, this year’s event aims to empower the youth with insights into current tech trends through expert-led talks and innovation-driven competitions. Students will not only learn about the technologies behind smart cities but also have the opportunity to share their own ideas and creativity.

Objectives

Encourage learning and discovery about the latest smart city technologies and trends.

Promote ethical discussions on data use, focusing on privacy, consent, fairness, bias, and accountability.

Equip students and professionals with education, training, and resources to work with smart city tech.

Inspire creativity and collaboration in both arts and tech within the smart city context.
