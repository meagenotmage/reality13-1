About RealITy

What is RealITy

RealITy Festival or RealITy is an annual tech rally by LINK.exe under the Pillar of Program. It features speakers from the tech industry and showcases cutting-edge innovations. The event connects technology trends with society, giving students and professionals a space to explore, learn, and get inspired by the latest in tech.

RealITy XIII: Metropolis

Now in its 13th year, RealITy focuses on smart cities with the theme METROPOLIS, inspired by Iloilo Cityâ€™s digital transformation. The event empowers youth through expert talks and innovation-driven contests, giving students a chance to explore tech trends and showcase their own smart city ideas.

Objectives

RealITy XIII promotes learning and discovery in smart city tech, encourages ethical discussions on data use, empowers participants through training and resources, and inspires creativity through collaborative, innovative platforms.
