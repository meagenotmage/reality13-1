About RealITy

What is RealITy

RealITy is WVSU's premier IT event that showcases innovative technological solutions and provides a platform for students to demonstrate their skills in various computing disciplines.

RealITy XIII: Metropolis

This year's theme "Metropolis" represents the convergence of technology and urban innovation, exploring how IT solutions can create smarter, more sustainable cities and communities.

Objectives

Our goal is to foster innovation, promote collaboration among IT professionals and students, and showcase cutting-edge solutions that address real-world problems in our increasingly digital society.
