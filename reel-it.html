QUALIFICATIONS

The contest is open to all College Content Creators and enthusiasts.

LINK.exe members and officers are NOT eligible to participate.

Interested participants may pre-register online via the provided Google Form or register on the day of the event by filling out the Registration Form.

Walk-in registrations are allowed.

GUIDELINES

Entries must not be published publicly before the awarding of the winners. Any footage found in the reel that doesn't follow these rules will result in the submission not being eligible.

Event-Day Presence

The contestant must be present within the day of the contest, May 8, 2025, onsite or else the contestant is disqualified.

Footage Requirements

The footage in the reel must be taken ONLY within the day of the event, RealITy XIII: METROPOLIS, and ONLY within the campus.

Time Requirements

Entries must have a minimum length of one (1) minute and a maximum length of two (2) minutes.

Content Type

Contestants may create either a Creative Reel or an Interview Reel. Only English or Tagalog language is allowed.

Interview Protocol

Interviews may only be conducted with students, faculty, or event attendees. Contestants must not disrupt the ongoing event.

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

Any type of filming device may be used.
Video resolution must be 1080 × 1920 px (9:16 aspect ratio).
File format: MP4 (H.264 encoding).
File name format: REALITY13_ReelsTitle_FullName

THEMES

Participants may use the hashtag: #RealITyXIII or #Metropolis in their videos

ADDITIONAL GUIDELINES

Minor post-processing is allowed, limited only to:

  • Contestants are encouraged to use creative editing techniques such as color grading, transitions, background music, and text overlays—as long as they enhance the clarity and storytelling of the reel.
  • Use only royalty-free or original music and media to avoid copyright issues.
  • Editing should not mislead or misrepresent people, events, or interviews.
  • Obtain consent from individuals who are clearly featured or interviewed.
  • Respect the privacy, dignity, and rights of all participants.
  • Content must not include anything offensive, discriminatory, or misleading.

Originalty and Intellectual Property

  • All entries must be original work of the contestant.
  • Submissions must not contain plagiarized material or use content owned by others without permission (e.g., copyrighted music, video clips, or images).
  • Any use of third-party assets (e.g., royalty-free music, stock footage) must be properly credited and compliant with their usage terms.
  • Contestants retain ownership of their work, but by submitting, they grant LINK.exe the right to use the video for promotional purposes, with proper credit.

Submission Details

  • Participants must submit their entries personally via Flash Drive to any technical committee member of LINK.exe on duty.
  • Each submission must include:
    The FINAL REEL OUTPUT (following technical specifications).
    File Name Format: REALITY13_ReelsTitle_FullName
  • Participants must compile their three (3) photos in a Google Document, with each photo followed by its corresponding caption placed directly below it.
  • Once completed, the Google Document must be downloaded as a PDF file and uploaded via the provided Google Form submission link.
  • In addition to the PDF, participants must also upload the original JPEG/JPG files of the three (3) photos in the same Google Form.
