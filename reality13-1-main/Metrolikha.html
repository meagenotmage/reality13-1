About the METROLIKHA

METROLIKHA is a digital art contest open to all digital artists and enthusiasts. Participants will create artwork that expresses creativity and innovation, focused on future cities and technological advancement.

This competition provides a platform for creative minds to showcase their artistic talents and digital skills, creating artwork that reflects the theme of modern urban development and technology.

Eligibility

Qualifications

The contest is open to all Digital Artists and enthusiasts from other universities or colleges.

LINK.exe members and officers are NOT eligible to participate.

Interested participants must register through the provided Google Forms and submit their output.

TECHNICALITIES

All submitted entries must be created using digital tools and must fall under the following categories:

  • 2D or 3D Computer-Generated Graphics - includes digital paintings, illustrations, vector art, pixel art, 3D modeling, rendering, and sculpting.
  • Dynamic Painting - Entries may incorporate digital painting techniques that simulate traditional brushwork and textures. If allowed by content rules, slight dynamic elements (such as subtle animations) may be considered.
  • Original - All artworks must be 100% original, created solely by the artist. Plagiarized, traced, or AI-generated artworks based on existing works are strictly prohibited.

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

Artworks must be created using any digital software of the contestant's choice.

Entries must follow an A4 proportion (8.3 × 11.7 inches) in PORTRAIT orientation.

File format: PNG

File name format: REALITY13_ArtTitle_FullName

EDITING AND ETHICAL CONSIDERATIONS

Editing Guidelines

  • Artists may use post-processing or editing tools to enhance their work, but must ensure that edits stay true to the original vision and do not involve any elements that misrepresent their authorship.
  • Any form of hate speech, discrimination, or inappropriate symbolism will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Use of AI-generated images, traced artworks, or plagiarized content from any source is STRICTLY prohibited.
  • Any submission found violating these guidelines will be automatically disqualified.

ORIGINALITY & INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

Ownership and Rights

  • All entries must be the original work of the artist and must not infringe on any existing copyrights or intellectual property rights.
  • Entries must not be previously published or submitted in other contests or platforms.
  • By submitting an entry, participants grant LINK.exe and Reality XIII: METROPOLIS the non-exclusive right to use their artwork for promotional and documentation purposes (with proper credit to the artist).
  • Artists retain full ownership of their original work.

SUBMISSION

Submission Details

  • Participants must submit their entries via the official Google Form provided.
  • The final digital artwork (following the technical specifications) and a short written explanation (briefly describing the concept and inspiration behind the piece).
  • Deadline for submission: May 1, 2025, at 11:59 PM.
  • Late submissions will not be accepted, as the form will automatically close after the deadline.
  • The announcement and awarding of winners will be conducted during the closing Program at the WVSU Research Building on the day of the event. The awarding of REALITY XIII: METROPOLIS can also be viewed through the live stream on LINK.exe's Facebook Page (facebook.com/link.exeofficial) and REALITY XIII: METROPOLIS Facebook Page (facebook.com/realityxiiiofficial).
